The Sense of Touch
Tactility is one of the most important parts of our sensory systems, and it is especially important when grading loose-leaf black
tea. In Sri Lanka, black tea is sorted and graded depending on its appearance and size. For instance, Orange Pekoe (OP) is a term
for the highest grade of black tea consisting of many whole tea leaves of a specific size. Read our blog to find out what makes
OP special. Another tea grading is Broken Orange Pekoe (BOP) which consists of the dustings, or fannings, of OP tea which produces
a stronger brew.
